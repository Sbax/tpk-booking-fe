### Privacy Policy in accordance with the GDPR

To use our services, we inform you that we will collect and store your email address. These data will be processed in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR -- EU Regulation 2016/679).

- **Purpose of processing:** The email address will be used exclusively to manage your booking/service and to send you necessary communications.
- **Legal basis:** Data collection is necessary to provide the requested service.
- **Data retention:** Your data will be stored for the time necessary to complete the service and subsequently to fulfill any legal obligations.
- **Data subject's rights:** You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data, restrict processing, object, and request data portability. You can also withdraw your consent at any time.
- **Data transfer:** Your data will not be transferred to third parties without your explicit consent.
- **Data security:** We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal data.
